Features

The only PI platform built for FHWA and NEPA compliance — from first stakeholder contact to AI-drafted report

Built by a public involvement professional, for public involvement professionals. No generic CRM workarounds — just the workflow you already know, properly supported.

Stakeholder registry

A master contact list across all your projects with duplicate detection, bulk import, LEP/EJ flags, influence tiers, and full engagement history — linked across every project automatically.

Interaction logging

Log every call, email, field visit, and meeting with channel, sentiment, NEPA stage, and equity tracking. Supports anonymous contacts and syncs instantly between desktop and mobile.

Issues tracker

Track concerns from first contact to resolution. Link issues to interactions and stakeholders, assign priority and status, and export issues summaries directly to Word or PDF.

Commitments tracker

Every commitment your team makes gets logged, categorized across 15+ types, and tracked to fulfillment. Open and fulfilled commitments flow directly into your PI reports automatically.

Events & outreach log

Document every public meeting, open house, newsletter, and media release. The outreach log populates your PI report events section automatically — no double entry, no gaps in the record.

Email contacts panel

Compose project emails directly from the app. Filter recipients by report distribution group, open in your mail client, and log the send as an outreach event — all in one step.

Roster importer

Import stakeholder rosters from Excel in seconds. Map columns, detect duplicates across project boundaries, and auto-assign report distribution groups before a single contact is saved.

Project dashboard

At-a-glance project health: engagement pace, open commitments, NEPA checklist progress, sentiment trends, deliverable status, influence map, and stakeholder matrix — all in one view.

Client portal

Give your client a live, read-only window into their own project — engagement activity, deliverable progress, and outreach history — without a manual reporting cycle. Scoped so they see only their project, nothing else in your system.

Mobile field app

Log contacts, interactions, and issues from any phone — no app install required. Full stakeholder lookup, anonymous contact logging, and real-time sync with the desktop registry.

PI report generation

Generate branded Word documents from your live data — PI reports, issues summaries, PI compliance summaries, deliverable logs, and stakeholder matrices. Build reports from modular sections, including a live NEPA compliance section and a public comment response matrix, and keep an automatic archive of every report you export. What used to take half a day takes minutes.

FHWA/NEPA compliance

NEPA process stage tagging on every interaction, project-level NEPA classification (CE/EA/EIS), compliance checklists by classification, public comment log with response tracking, and comment period management — built to the current 23 CFR Part 771 standard.

Tribal consultation tracker In development

Log and track government-to-government tribal consultation activities, response deadlines, concurrence status, and consultation notes — with dashboard alerts for overdue responses.

LEP / Environmental Justice

Flag LEP and underserved stakeholders at the contact level. Dashboard metrics, PI compliance reports, and Word exports surface EJ outreach automatically — supporting Title VI and EO 12898 documentation requirements.

AI report drafting & trend analysis

Draft PI report narratives with one click. AI reads your logged interactions, concerns, outreach events, and commitments — and writes professional, FHWA-appropriate summaries ready to review and send. Once you've archived a few reports, generate an AI trend analysis across your project's full reporting history.

Built to WCAG 2.1 AA

Accessible by design — color contrast, keyboard focus states, screen-reader navigation, and live-region updates meet WCAG 2.1 AA, so your team's tools hold up to the same accessibility standard your agency work does.

ArcGIS Survey123 import In development

Pull public survey responses directly from ArcGIS Survey123 into Horizon COMPASS as interaction logs — eliminating manual re-entry and closing the gap between field outreach data collection and your internal PI record.

Hotline voicemail transcription In development

Project phone hotline voicemails automatically transcribed and logged as interaction records — with stakeholder matching, subject tagging, and follow-up flagging. No more manual transcription from the construction hotline.

PI Client Portal New

Give your DOT or municipal client a secure, read-only live view of public engagement activity and deliverable progress on their project — scoped to their project only. The transparency layer no other PI tool offers.

How it works

From first contact to FHWA-ready report

01

Set up your project

Create a project, set the NEPA classification (CE / EA / EIS), and import your contact roster in minutes. Duplicate detection and auto-assigned report distribution groups keep your registry clean from day one.

02

Log every interaction — with NEPA context

Desktop or mobile — your team logs calls, emails, meetings, and field contacts as they happen. Every interaction is tagged to a NEPA process stage, a stakeholder, and a project. LEP and Environmental Justice flags are built into every contact record.

03

Track compliance, issues, and commitments

Log public comments with comment periods and response tracking. Monitor tribal consultation deadlines. Record commitments across 15+ categories. Flag concerns as issues and track them to resolution — all in one place.

04

Draft your report with AI

Open the PI Report Editor and let AI draft your narrative sections from logged data — concerns, outreach summary, NEPA compliance status, trends. Review, edit, and refine. Export a branded Word document ready to submit.

05

Give your client a live window — not a quarterly PDF

The PI Client Portal gives your DOT, city, or county client a secure, read-only view of public engagement activity and deliverable progress on their specific project — updated in real time as your team logs work. No other PI platform offers this. Your client sees what they need; your internal workflow stays private.

Meet the founder

A career built on telling complex stories simply

Jeff Putzke
Founder / CEO
LinkedIn →

Horizon COMPASS exists because Jeff Putzke has lived every side of public communication — and saw firsthand how much of it still runs on spreadsheets, sticky notes, and tribal knowledge. His career started not in stakeholder engagement, but in code. As a computer programmer for St. Paul Regional Water Services in the mid-1990s, Jeff learned how to design systems that solve real, repeatable problems. That foundation in software thinking never left him.

From there, his path took an unusual turn. Armed with a Bachelor of Science in Meteorology from St. Cloud State University, Jeff spent the next 15+ years as a broadcast meteorologist across six major television markets — Minneapolis, Omaha, Traverse City, Salt Lake City (KTVX ABC4), Tucson (Chief Meteorologist, KGUN 9), and Sacramento (KOVR/CBS) — and continued as a freelance meteorologist in the Salt Lake market through 2022. Live television taught him what an audience needs: clear narratives, plain language, and the ability to distill complex technical information into something a viewer cares about in under thirty seconds. Mass engagement, public speaking, and storytelling design became second nature.

Between broadcasting and his current role, Jeff spent several years as a sales and design engineer for a SaaS clinical research software company, where he learned the discipline of translating user needs into software features, scoping product requirements with stakeholders, and selling technical solutions to non-technical buyers. That experience would later prove foundational to the way Horizon COMPASS was designed — built from the inside out, by someone who has sat on both sides of the demo.

In 2019, Jeff joined Sunrise Engineering and built the Public Involvement practice from a one-person specialty into a full team serving UDOT, municipal, and utility projects across Utah. Along the way, he worked on dozens of high-profile transportation and infrastructure communications efforts. Horizon COMPASS is the natural product of that arc — software engineering instincts, SaaS product sensibility, broadcast-honed clarity, and a deep understanding of what PI teams actually do every day, built into one tool by someone who has lived the work.
